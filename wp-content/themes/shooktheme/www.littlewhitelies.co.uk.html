Little White Lies recommends...

  1. Met Opera Live On Saturday the Barbican continues its high-def hook-up with the New York Met, screening Massenet's Thais. Sounds cultural.
  2. White Christmas Buffet Stuck for Christmas Eve plans? Head to the City Screen in York for a screening of this festive classic and free food to match.
  3. The Bicycle Thieves The film of the week is this re-mastered re-release of Vittorio de Sica's masterpiece about a man who has his biked pinched in post-war Rome.
  4. Man on Wire Our ex-cover movie and one of the films of the year comes to DVD. The story of Philippe Petit's wire-walk across the World Trade Center towers is the stuff that cinema was made for.
  5. Rouben Mamoulian Free screening of classic shorts followed by The Cabinet of Dr. Caligari on Sunday in that well-known film Mecca of Norwich.
